Mission

There are lots of apps for planning and scheduling.

Our app allows you to take advantage of your friends and your downtime by (re)connecting you with your friends through conversation.

Whether its 5 minutes or a few hours, take that extra time to talk on the phone with a friend that is available now!

Description

REconnect allows people to create groups and get notified that they are available to catch up on the phone for a specified period of time. In a modern world of text-only communication with ambiguity of meanings and no assurance of a response, wouldn't it be nice to know who you could call on the phone without needing to know their schedule or feeling like you are going to bother them? You want to catch up, but you don't want to spend 20 minutes calling five or six people to see if they are free to talk.
We have got you covered!

Motivation

Have you ever wanted to talk to your friends but dont know when they are availible

or,

spend more time calling people in your contact list and no one answers, then by the time you finally connect with someone you cant talk for long?

We have and we wanted to make something that we could use to reconnect with our friends!

Minimum Viable Product

This application registers a new user, allows existing users to log in, after logging in the user can create a new groups, search for members, allow users to add members or delete members from groups, set your current availability, and send notifications for people currently available.

Results

Registration, Sign Up, Create Groups, Get and Send Notificaitons
